# Privacy Policy — ASM Reference

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview
ASM Reference does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
This app collects no data whatsoever. There is no account creation, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

## How the App Works
ASM Reference functions entirely offline. All clinical content is built into the app. Nothing leaves your device.

## Third Parties
No third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are used.

## Children
This app is intended for healthcare professionals and does not target children.
